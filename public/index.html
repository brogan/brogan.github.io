Why the title?

A bit of pedantry.

  • Latin i (ifer) = connective vowel
  • Latin ferre = to bear, carry
  • Latin osus, Old French ous = full of, characterised by, bearing

So Broganiferous = bearing the quality of Brogan (that’s me).

Which may well be unbearable.
